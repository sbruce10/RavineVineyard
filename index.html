Although we're sure that every farm family feels the same about their 34 acre organic winery, at Ravine, we believe that ours is truly unique.

Perhaps it's because of our approach to family. Our goal was to create a sustainable way to maintain our 100+ year old farm and continue to foster the legacy of our interest, follow dreams and find ways to continue to fall in love - with the land and each other.

In that we have learned a lot, attracted some great people and extended our passionate family. The result is not only great wines from our mother, Lowrey Farm, but a wonderful lifestyle that we are eager and proud to share.

The wines at Ravine Vineyard are often desribed as "elegant" or "refined". This style is as much a result of the terroir as it is of a unique winemaking effort.

In the cellar, care and gentle guidance of the wines prevails during the entire winemaking process. Gravity flow is used throughout because it is very gentle on the juices and wines. All grapes are hand harvested and hand sorted on a state of the art, vibrating sorting table before going to the press or crusher destemmer.

The Wood Oven

The wood oven at Ravine is at the heart of the property. It produces our daily bread baked by our artisanal break baker, Andres Grieves. A day in the life of the oven will also include roasting vegetables, braising meets, baking pies and many friends enjoying fired pizzas while sipping our estate wine…

Fresh from our Garden

At Ravine not only is our entire vineyard certified organic, but also our ¾ acre vegetable garden is as well. Our garden is lovingly planted and cared for by our resident gardener with “the green thumbs.”

Another strong garden partnership we cherish is with Wachs Farms. Paul Wachs tailor grows many of the vegetables used in our seasonal menus, harvested and delivered to the back door at the peek of their ripeness.
